# Making Time Editable in Video Diffusion Transformers

- **arXiv:** 2606.10183 — https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10183
- **PDF (blocked this run by egress policy):** https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.10183
- **Date:** 2026-06-08
- **Subfield:** Controllable video / DiT

## Summary (as retrieved via web search)
Proposes a temporal-control methodology that extends a pretrained video Diffusion Transformer (DiT) with
explicit "time editing", allowing control over motion speed and temporal structure of generated video without
retraining the base model. Targets the gap where spatial/semantic controllability in DiT video models has
matured faster than fine-grained control over time (slow motion, speed ramps, re-timing).
