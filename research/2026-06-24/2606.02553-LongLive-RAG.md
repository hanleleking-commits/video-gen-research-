## Summary (as retrieved via web search)
Autoregressive (AR) video diffusion enables variable-length synthesis, but long-horizon generation often
suffers from accumulated errors and identity drift. LongLive-RAG introduces retrieval-augmented generation as
a general framework for long video: it retrieves and conditions on previously generated context to anchor
identity and reduce drift over long horizons. Complements the global-state / explicit-memory line of work
(e.g. VRAG, RELIC).
