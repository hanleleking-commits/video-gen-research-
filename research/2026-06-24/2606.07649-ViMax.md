## Summary (as retrieved via web search)
ViMax is an agentic video-generation framework for long-form video, which requires systematic narrative
planning and visual consistency that short-clip methods cannot provide. It addresses video creation through
coordinated multi-agent collaboration in which specialized components — director, screenwriter, producer,
generator — negotiate narrative decisions, visual continuity, and production quality.
