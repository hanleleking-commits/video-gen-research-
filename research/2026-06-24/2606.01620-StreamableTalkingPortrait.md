## Summary (as retrieved via web search)
A framework for streamable talking-portrait video generation conditioned on speech audio and a reference
image, designed for streaming scenarios. It uses a causal video VAE for deep latent compression together with
an autoregressive latent denoising model, enabling low-latency frame-by-frame production suitable for
real-time streaming.
